Features功能

Everything you need,
nothing you don't. 只做一件事，
做到极致。

No Electron. No background service. No subscription. A single 83KB binary that does exactly one thing perfectly. 没有 Electron，没有后台服务，没有订阅。一个 83KB 的原生二进制文件，只做一件事。

One-Click Sleep Prevention 一键防休眠

Disables system sleep entirely — including lid close. Your AI agents, long builds, and overnight downloads keep running without interruption. 完全禁用系统休眠，包括合盖。你的 AI Agent、长时间编译、通宵下载都不会中断。

Smart Battery Protection 智能电池保护

Monitors battery every 60 seconds. Lid closed at ≤5% → auto sleep to protect hardware. Lid open → notification only. Warning at 10%. 每 60 秒监控电量。合盖且电量 ≤5% → 自动休眠保护硬件。开盖 → 仅推送通知。10% 时发出警告。

Zero-Config Privileges 零配置权限

First launch triggers the native macOS password dialog. One password entry, forever passwordless. Only grants access to pmset — nothing else. 首次启动弹出 macOS 原生密码框，输入一次密码即可永久免密。仅授权 pmset 命令，不涉及其他权限。

Menu Bar Native 原生菜单栏

Lives in your menu bar with SF Symbols and live battery percentage. Toggle on/off, launch at login. No Dock icon, no clutter. SF Symbols 图标常驻菜单栏，实时显示电量百分比。开关切换，支持开机自启。无 Dock 图标，不占空间。

Setup安装

Three steps. Under a minute. 三步完成，不到一分钟。

No terminal. No Homebrew. No Xcode. 不需要终端、Homebrew 或 Xcode。

01

Download & Open下载并打开

Unzip AlwaysOn.zip and drag the app to Applications. First launch: right-click → Open. 解压 AlwaysOn.zip，将 App 拖入应用程序文件夹。首次启动：右键 → 打开。

02

Grant Permission授予权限

A native macOS dialog asks for your password — once. This configures passwordless access for the pmset command only. macOS 原生密码弹窗会要求输入密码 — 仅此一次。这将配置 pmset 命令的免密执行权限。

03

Click Enable点击启用

Hit "Enable AlwaysOn" from the menu bar. Done. Your Mac stays awake with the lid closed, WiFi connected, all processes running. 点击菜单栏中的"Enable AlwaysOn"即可。Mac 合盖后保持唤醒，WiFi 在线，所有进程继续运行。

The app always tries to restore defaults on quit, but force-quit bypasses that. 防休眠状态会保持，直到你重新打开 AlwaysOn 点击 "Disable"，或在终端手动执行 sudo pmset -a disablesleep 0。正常退出时 App 会自动恢复默认设置，但强制退出会跳过这一步。
